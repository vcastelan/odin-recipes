Peanut Butter and Jelly Recipe


How to make a Peanut butter Sandwich

In order to make a pb&j sandwich we need 2 bread slices, some peanut butter, jelly and spoon.

Ingredients

  • 2 bread slices
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly
  • Spoon

Steps to make

  1. Spread some peanut butter using the spoon on one slice of bread
  2. Then spread some jelly using the same or another spoon on the other slice of bread
  3. Bring both slices together. Ready to eat!
